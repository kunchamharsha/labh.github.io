About Us

Labh is an advanced financial research and wealth solutions platform built for the modern Indian investor. We combine deep quantitative research, rigorous fundamental analysis, and technology-driven execution to help investors make smarter, more disciplined investment decisions.

Founded by professionals with experience across finance, engineering, and large-scale technology platforms, Labh was created to bridge a critical gap in the Indian investment ecosystem — institution-grade research made accessible to retail investors.

Our team includes PhD mathematicians, CFA Level 3 professionals, qualified accountants, and experienced engineers, working together to design robust portfolio strategies focused on long-term wealth creation and effective risk management.

What We Do

At Labh, we specialize in curated investment baskets designed using a structured, data-backed approach:

  • Stock Baskets – Research-driven equity portfolios built using quantitative models, fundamental screening, and disciplined risk controls.
  • Mutual Fund Baskets – Professionally constructed mutual fund portfolios optimized for asset allocation, diversification, and long-term consistency.

Each basket is continuously monitored and refined using advanced analytics, market data, and systematic portfolio management frameworks.

Our Philosophy

We believe successful investing is not about short-term predictions or market noise, but about building a repeatable and disciplined process. Our core principles include:

  • Process over emotions
  • Risk management over return chasing
  • Consistency over speculation

Our research frameworks emphasize downside protection, diversification, and probabilistic thinking — principles commonly used by institutional investors but rarely available to retail participants.

Built for Scale, Trust, and Transparency

Labh is built on a robust technology stack designed to deliver a seamless, reliable, and scalable experience. We operate with a strong focus on regulatory compliance, transparency, and investor education, ensuring users always understand the rationale, risks, and objectives behind every strategy.

Our Mission

To empower Indian investors with research-first, unbiased, and data-driven investment solutions that stand the test of time.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted platform for systematic, research-backed wealth creation.
