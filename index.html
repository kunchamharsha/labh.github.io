How can Labh help me invest?

Labh takes the guesswork out of investing. We analyze the market, pick the best options for you, and handle all the nitty-gritty details. You get to focus on your goals while we work behind the scenes to make your money work for you!

How safe is my Money?

With Labh, your money is in secure hands. All transactions are processed directly through NSE and BSE, India’s most trusted stock exchanges, and your securities go straight into your personal Demat Account. At no point does your money enter our bank account, ensuring complete control and security over your investments.

Why Should I Invest with Labh?

Labh doesn’t just help you grow your wealth—we help protect it, too. With real-time tracking and advanced monitoring, we keep your investments aligned with market trends and spot new opportunities for you.
And if a rare “black swan” event is on the horizon, we’re ready to act fast, helping you avoid losses before they happen. With Labh, you get the advantage of proactive, secure investing—so you never miss an opportunity or get caught off-guard.

How Do You Charge?

Labh offers two types of services: Stock Baskets and Mutual Fund Baskets.

  • Mutual Fund Baskets: We earn a commission of 0.75% from the Asset Management Company (AMC) for your investments, which is set by SEBI.

  • Stock Baskets: We charge a yearly subscription fee for access to our expertly curated stock selections.

With Labh, you get transparent pricing designed to keep your investment costs clear and manageable!

How does Labh keep my investments safe?

At Labh, your security is our priority. We use top-tier hosting with DigitalOcean, ensuring a robust infrastructure. Our data is protected through regular security audits, encryption, and DDoS protection.
We also implement strict access controls and maintain backup systems to safeguard your investments.
With Labh, you can invest confidently, knowing we’re committed to keeping your information secure.
