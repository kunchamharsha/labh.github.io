F A Q's

Labh is a research organisation that researches and creates baskets of mutual funds and stocks that will yield optimal returns according to your risk appetite.

Your orders are processed through NSE and BSE, the two largest stock exchanges in our country. The securities will be deposited in your Demat Account. At no point will your money touch our bank account.

Investing is a high-consequence activity. It requires constant tracking of your assets with respect to market conditions. Labh has the technical resources to help monitor, keep track of threats, and identify opportunities. In case of black swan events, we will pull you out before you go down hard.

We have two types of services: Stock baskets and Mutual Fund baskets.
For mutual funds, we get 0.75% commissions from the AMC (set by SEBI) for the investments you make.
For stock baskets, we charge you on a yearly subscription basis.

  • For infrastructure security, we host our services on DigitalOcean, which is AICPA SOC 2 Type II and SOC 3 Type II certified.
  • We perform regular VAPT audits and follow best security practices.
  • All critical personally identifiable data is encrypted with 256-bit encryption.
  • We use Cloudflare DDOS protection to secure the system from DDOS attacks.
  • We have access-based controls to limit the data accessed by our teams.
  • Our systems feature continuous backup mechanisms to prevent data loss in cases of disaster.
