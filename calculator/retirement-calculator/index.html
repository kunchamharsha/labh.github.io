Formula

$$ \text{SIP} = \frac{[C \times (1 + r)^t \times 12 \times y] \times i}{(1 + i)^n - 1} $$

C Monthly expenses
r Expected annual rate of return (interest).
t Years until retirement
y Years expected after retirement
i Monthly return rate (e.g., 12% annual = 0.01 monthly)
n Total number of months to invest (n = t × 12)
