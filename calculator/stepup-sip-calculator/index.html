Formula

$$ \text{Future Value} = P \cdot \left( \frac{(1 + r)^n - 1}{r} \cdot (1 + r) \right) + Q \cdot \left( \frac{(1 + r)^n - (1 + i)^n}{r - i} \cdot (1 + r) \right) $$

P Fixed payment each period
r Monthly interest rate (annual interest rate divided by 12).
n Total number of monthly installments (loan tenure in months)
Q Increasing payment added every period

FAQ's

What is a step-up SIP?

It’s a SIP where you increase your investment amount annually by a fixed percentage or amount.

Can I stop a step-up SIP anytime?

Yes, you can pause or stop it, but check with your mutual fund provider for penalties.

IHow does a step-up SIP help in long-term goals?

It allows you to invest more as your income grows, helping you achieve bigger financial goals.
