How to Use?

  • Enter your investment amount.
  • Get quarterly interest payout values.

Formula

$$ \text{Quarterly Interest} = \frac{P \times R}{4 \times 100} $$ $$ \text{Total Interest} = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100} $$

P : Principal amount.
r : Expected Quarterly return rate.
T : Tenure

Advantages

  • Tax benefit under 80C
