How to Use?

  • Enter basic monthly salary, current age, retirement age, and current PF balance.

Formula

$$ PF = \sum_{y=0}^{Y-1} \sum_{m=1}^{12} \left( PF_{y,m-1} \times \left(1 + \frac{R}{12} \right) + S_0 \times (1 + i)^y \times r \right) $$

$$ PF_{y,m-1} $$ PF balance at previous month
$$ S_0 $$ Initial monthly salary
r Contribution percentage (as decimal)
i Annual salary increment percentage (as decimal)
R Annual interest rate (as decimal)

Advantages

  • Easy tracking of retirement corpus
  • Clarity on employer and employee contributions
  • Future planning simplified
  • Encourages awareness of PF benefits
