How to Use?

  • Input your child's current age and expected age at college.

Formula

FAQ's

What if I don’t know the current cost of education?

Research the average cost of the course or use an estimated figure.

How do I adjust for inflation?

Use an average education inflation rate (e.g., 10-12%).
