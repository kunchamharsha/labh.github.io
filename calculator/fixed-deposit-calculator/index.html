How to Use?

  • Input deposit amount, interest rate, and tenure.
  • Select compounding frequency.
  • View maturity amount and total interest.

Formula

A = P (1 + r/n)^(nt)

A The maturity amount or total amount after investment.
P The principal amount or initial deposit.
r Annual interest rate (in decimal form).
n Number of times interest is compounded per year.
t Investment duration in years.

Advantages

  • Clarity on returns
  • Easy comparison among banks
  • Flexible tenure planning
  • Interest compounding visibility
