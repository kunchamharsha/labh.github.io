Formula

$$ A = P \times \frac{(1 + r)^n - 1}{r} \times (1 + r) $$

$$ A $$ Maturity amount
$$ P $$ Annual payment made at the beginning of each year
$$ r $$ Annual interest rate (in decimal)
$$ n $$ Number of years

Advantages

  • Accurately estimates your PPF maturity amount based on your inputs.
