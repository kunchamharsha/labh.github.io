What are Mutual Funds?

A mutual fund is a professionally managed investment pool that allows investors to access a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, and other securities.

Types of Mutual Funds?

Not All Mutual Funds Are The Same. Here Are The Main Types You Should Know

Equity Funds

Invest In Company Shares, Higher Growth, Higher Risk.

Debt Funds

Invest In Bonds, Stable And Lower Risk.

Hybrid Funds

Mix Of Equity And Debt For Balanced Returns.

ELSS Funds

Tax-Saving Equity Funds Eligible Under Section 80C.

What are Mutual Fund Baskets?

Mutual fund baskets are ready-made portfolios built with multiple high-performing funds for different goals and risk levels. At Labh, these baskets are built using Advanced Quant Technology that analyses 35,000+ mutual fund schemes and selects strong performers using PhD-led, 16-year back tested models.
